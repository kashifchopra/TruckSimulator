Products Offered

Trading in Equities at NSE is available on NEAT, NOW and ODIN CTCL System

Trading in Derivatives at NSE is available on NEAT, NOW and ODIN CTCL System

Trading in Currency Derivatives at NSE is available on NEAT, NOW and ODIN CTCL System

Trading in Equities at BSE is available on BOLT and ODIN CTCL System

Services Offered

Internet Trading (On Broadband)

Divya provides a high-end, integrated, multi-segment, and user-friendly internet trading applications to the clients via NOW and ODIN CTCL Platform; these provide quotes on a real time basis in addition to a large number of enhanced trading features.

Leaseline NSE

A lease line facility provided directly by NSE. It is one of the fastest connectivity modes available and ideally suited for day trading and jobbing purposes.

VSAT NSE/BSE

The oldest and time tested connectivity provided by the NSE. Reliability of this network is very high; however, as compared to other means of connectivity costing is slightly on the higher side.
